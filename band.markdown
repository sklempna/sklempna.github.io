## Cerebral Invasion is Born from pure hate.

After numerous disappointments in his life, singer Mahmood's frustration reached an unbearable level. Instead of losing his mind, he decided to put together a band.

Cerebral Invasion play mercyless Thrash Metal and are based in the Ruhr-area, Germany.

Cerebral invasion is (from left to right):

- Kilian (drums)
- Dominik (guitar)
- Mahmood (vocals)
- Absorber (guitar)
- Olli (bass) (former member)
